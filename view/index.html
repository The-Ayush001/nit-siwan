    • Government engineering college siwan is a premier institution of Bihar Government under Department of Science and Technology. It is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and is affiliated to Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna. The college is established in 2018 and is situated in Siwan district of Bihar. The college is spread over 25 acres of land. The college is committed to provide quality technical education to the students. The college has well equipped laboratories, workshops, library, computer center, seminar hall, auditorium, sports facilities, hostels and other facilities. The college has well qualified and experienced faculty members. The college offers B.Tech. degree in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Computer Science and Engineering. The college has a Training and Placement Cell which provides training and placement opportunities to the students. The college has a strong alumni network. The college has a strong industry-institute interaction. The college has a strong research and development cell. The college has a strong entrepreneurship development cell. The college has a strong innovation and incubation cell. The college has a strong social responsibility cell. The college has a strong sports and cultural cell.

    Welcome to GEC Siwan

    GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE(GEC), SIWAN is one of the new Government Engineering College established by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science and Technology. It will start its first academic session 2019 from campus of Government Polytechnic Siwan, Bihar. The new engineering college will have three engineering branches –Civil, Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, with an intake of 60, 60 and 120 per branch respectively and 240 students in total.
