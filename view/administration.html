Adiministration

The administration of the college is responsible for the overall management and operation of the institution. This includes overseeing academic programs, student services, and administrative functions. The administration works to ensure that the college runs smoothly and efficiently, and that all students have access to the resources and support they need to succeed. The administration also plays a key role in setting the strategic direction of the institution and working to achieve its goals and objectives.

Principal

The Principal is the head of the college and is responsible for the overall management and administration of the institution. They ensure that the college runs smoothly and efficiently, and that all academic and administrative processes are carried out effectively. The Principal also represents the college in various forums and is the key decision-maker in all matters related to the institution.

Dean of Academics

The Dean of Academics is responsible for overseeing the academic programs of the college. They work closely with the faculty to develop and implement the curriculum, and ensure that the academic standards of the institution are maintained. The Dean of Academics also plays a key role in student advising and support, and works to enhance the overall academic experience of the students.



Registrar

The Registrar is responsible for managing the administrative functions of the college. They oversee student registration and enrollment, maintain student records, and coordinate the academic calendar. The Registrar also plays a key role in ensuring compliance with college policies and procedures, and works to support the overall mission and goals of the institution.

Director of Student Affairs

The Director of Student Affairs is responsible for overseeing the non-academic programs and services of the college. They work to enhance the overall student experience by providing support and resources to help students succeed. The Director of Student Affairs also plays a key role in student engagement and retention, and works to create a positive and inclusive campus environment.

GEC SIWAN

Government Engineering College, Siwan is a government engineering college in Siwan, Bihar, India. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and affiliated with Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna.
