About the Central Library

The Central Library at Government Engineering College, Siwan, serves as the academic heart of the institution, providing a vast repository of knowledge to support the educational and research needs of our students, faculty, and staff. Our mission is to foster a culture of learning and intellectual curiosity by offering a diverse collection of resources and state-of-the-art facilities. We are committed to providing a conducive environment for study and research, enabling academic excellence and professional growth.

Our Vision

To be a leading resource center that empowers the GEC Siwan community with innovative services and comprehensive information, driving research and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To acquire, organize, and disseminate information resources effectively while providing a welcoming and tech-enabled space for collaborative and individual study.

Library Collections

Print Collection

A comprehensive collection of textbooks, reference books, and journals spanning all engineering disciplines and general knowledge.

Digital Resources

Access to a wide range of e-books, e-journals, and online databases, providing 24/7 access to scholarly information.
